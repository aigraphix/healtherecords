✦ Capabilities

Everything You Need to
Manage Your Health

Built with Expo, Supabase, and Gemini AI to give patients and doctors a unified, secure, and intelligent health platform.

💬

Agentic Chat

Speak naturally — the AI classifies your intent, prepares the action, and confirms before writing. Book appointments, send messages, refill prescriptions, all by chatting.

📷

Smart Scanner

Unified camera scanner auto-detects barcodes and nutrition labels. Gemini Flash analyzes documents and images, extracting health-relevant information automatically.

🔌

Offline Logging

Log vitals offline — "BP 120/80", "Weight 185 lbs". Regex-based parser works without AI. SQLite queue syncs to Supabase when back online. 12 log types supported.

🎥

Telehealth

LiveKit-powered real-time video appointments with doctors. Join your appointment from the chat — opens 10 minutes before the scheduled time.

📄

Document Management

Upload, view, and share medical documents via Supabase Storage. PDFs, images, and lab results — all secured with row-level security and signed URLs.

🌐

9 Languages

Full i18n support with react-i18next. English, Spanish, French, Italian, Japanese, Polish, Russian, Ukrainian, and Chinese (Simplified).

🏗 Architecture

Built on a Modern Stack

Every layer is purpose-built for security, performance, and developer experience.

Technology Stack

📱 Frontend React Native 0.83 (Expo Router v5)
🗄 Backend Supabase (Postgres + RLS + Edge Fns)
🤖 AI Google Gemini Flash (gemini-2.5-flash)
📦 Storage Supabase Storage (signed URLs)
🔌 Offline expo-sqlite + sync engine
🔐 Auth Supabase Auth (email, Apple Sign-In)
🎥 Video LiveKit WebRTC
⚡ Runtime Hermes JS, New Architecture (SDK 55)
🛡️ HIPAA Compliant

Protected Health Information (PHI) is encrypted in transit and at rest. Row-level security ensures patients only see their own data. Doctors require explicit patient authorization.

data-i18n="sec.e2ee"> 🔒 End-to-End Encryption

Messages are encrypted on-device before transmission. Only authorized recipients can decrypt content. Encryption keys are never exposed in the UI.

🔍 Security Gate CI

Automated GitHub Actions pipeline runs RLS policy probes, JWT abuse tests, secrets scanning, and adversarial simulations on every push.

🌍 Multilingual

Speaks Your Language

Full translation coverage across 9 languages with automatic language detection and in-app translation for health logs.
