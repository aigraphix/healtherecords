End-to-End Encryption

Effective: November 21, 2025 Contact: apd1034@gmail.com

End-to-end encryption (E2EE) means message content is encrypted on your device and can only be decrypted by the intended recipients.

  • On-device encryption: content is encrypted before it leaves your device.
  • Recipient-only decryption: only authorized recipients can decrypt the content.
  • Key protection: encryption keys are protected and not shown in the UI.
  • Metadata limits: message content is encrypted, some metadata may remain.
  • Sharing control: access depends on doctor/patient authorization.

Encrypted content is treated as opaque data during transport and storage.
